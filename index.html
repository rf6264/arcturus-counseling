Welcome to Arcturus

A Brighter Path Forward

At Arcturus Counseling & Wellness, we provide a safe, compassionate space for individuals, couples, and families seeking healing, growth, and renewed hope. Located in Litchfield Park, Arizona, we proudly serve clients throughout the West Valley. Our mission is to guide you on your journey toward emotional well-being and a more fulfilling life.

We also support clients navigating trauma recovery, grief and loss, Christian counseling, life transitions, personal development, OCD, anxiety disorders, bipolar disorder, postpartum depression, and other mental health challenges using evidence-based approaches such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Internal Family Systems (IFS) therapy.


Services are provided by Regina McManigell, LAC, under clinical supervision of David Phelan, LPC-2307.

How We Can Help

Therapy for Life’s Journey

Marriage, Relationship, & Couples Counseling

Whether you're facing communication challenges, relationship conflict, infidelity, or feeling disconnected from your partner, counseling can help you rebuild trust, strengthen your connection, and move forward together with greater understanding and confidence.

Anxiety & Depression Therapy

Find relief from anxiety, depression, panic, excessive worry, and overwhelming stress. Using evidence-based approaches such as CBT and other proven therapeutic techniques, we'll work together to build healthier coping skills and greater emotional resilience.

Anger Management

Uncontrolled anger can impact your relationships, work, and overall quality of life. Anger management can help you process past difficulties, identify triggers, improve emotional regulation, and develop healthier ways to respond to stress and conflict.
